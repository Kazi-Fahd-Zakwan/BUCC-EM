About EM Department

Event Management (EM) department of BRAC University Computer Club (BUCC) is one of the six departments which collaboratively work in order to organize events like workshops, tech sessions, seminars, programming contests, short tours, orientations and so on. The department consists of Directors, Assistant Directors, Seniors Executives, Executives and General Members who consistently participate in the working process of the club. Some of the major duties Event Management fulfills are venue booking, food allocations and distributions, guest entry permission, photography managing, photo storing and most importantly managing any necessary equipment required for an event. The other five departments of the club are Creative, Human Resource, Press Release, Finance and Marketing. Pre-Event Activities

There are certain tasks which Event Management Department needs to complete before an event

  • Venue/Room Booking: Room/venue booking of the particular event must be done by EM. If the venue is at UB4, UB6, UB7 or UB8, then EM needs to mail the DCO of CSE department in order to book the room. In addition, for venues which are situated at UB1, UB2, UB3 or UB5, EM needs to collect the room booking form from Office of Student Life (OSL), afterwards fill it up with necessary information about the event and submit it at the registers office. In both cases, EM needs to confirm the venue after requesting for it.

  • Guest ID: EM needs to collect Guest ID from the guards table at ground floor of the university building where the event is going to be held. For collecting it, they must show the proposal letter of that event which must be signed by OSL/CSE department and Register. It is needless to mention that, without the guest ID, guests can't enter the university building at any cost.

  • Necessary Requirements: EM needs to manage all the necessary requirements of an event. Requirements include food, projector, marker, X-banner, crest, certificate etc. In case any item needs to be brought from the shop, remember to collect the receipt. To know about the requirements of an event, EM must go through the proposal letter thoroughly because requirements must be included in the proposal letter. It is the responsibility of EM to bring the required things before the event.

  • Photographers: The photographer selection process of an event is supervised by EM. Generally, one or two photographers are selected for an particular event who covers the event. In rare cases where the photographer doesn't have a DSLR camera, EM needs to manage the camera from other photographers who has DSLR camera.

  • EM Representatives: Before an event, EM needs to select volunteers from EM department who are going to represent the department during the event. Typically, two to four volunteers are selected for an particular event who would represent EM department at the event. The volunteers have huge responsibilities during the event which will be discussed in the on-event activities.

On-Event Activities

There are certain duties which Event Management Department needs to fulfill during an event

  • Be on time: The very fundamental responsibility of EM representatives is to be present at least fifteen to twenty minutes before the event starts. In addition, they must contact with the floor in charge and ensure everything is set to go. If there are students in the room where the event is going to occur, the floor in charge will inform the students about the event. Furthermore, the floor in charge will also check if all the required equipments like projector, desktop, sound system etc. are working properly. EM representatives will monitor these processes.

  • Guest Receive: EM representatives shall contact the guest and receive them from university premises and bring them to the event venue. They must also ensure that the guests have wore the guest ID while entering the university building.

  • Food Distribution: If there is a food allocation for the event, then EM representatives need to distribute the food among the guests and the participants of the event.

  • Situation Handling: Any incident that may occur during the event must be handled by the EM representatives. Situations like crowd handling must be controlled carefully by the EM representatives.

  • Collaborate: During an event, there will be representatives from other departments of BRAC University Computer Club. EM representatives need to collaborate with them and work together in order to make the event a success.

Post-Event Activities

There are certain responsibilities which Event Management Department needs to finish after an event

  • Guest ID Submission: After the event is over and the guests are leaving, EM representatives must collect and return the guest ID from where they collected it before the event.

  • Room Checking: After the event is finished, EM representatives should check the venue to see if any guest or participant has mistakenly left any of their stuffs. If any stuff is found, they should give it to the floor in charge.

  • Returning Equipments: If any additional equipment like projector, marker etc. was brought from the CSE department or OSL, then it should be returned by the EM representatives after the event.

  • Budget Preparation: In case there was a budget allocation for the event, EM needs to hand over the total bill along with all the receipts of the products brought to Finance Department so that they can submit the bill to the authority.

Photographers Responsibilities

  • A picture should be taken while trying to cover as much audience as possible. Always try to avoid taking single pictures of someone.

  • The pictures needs to be submitted on due time, to be more specific on the day of the event. If the photographer wants to edit a picture before submitting then he/she may do so otherwise it is better to submit the photos as soon as possible.

  • The photos must only be submitted to the President, Vice President, General Secretary and the Directors of EM department. It is recommended to upload the photos to Google drive and give the access to the above personals.

  • If anyone asks the photographer to give him/her their photo, the photographer should request him/her to ask the photo from the directors of EM department.

  • Photographers must take group photos at the end of the event.

  • Photographers must keep in mind to take several photos of the guests and faculties when they are giving speeches.

  • Photographers should try to click several pictures of the banners of the event.
